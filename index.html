Brand Positioning

Core Positioning

ChiveIQ is the unified operations platform for multi-unit QSR brands that replaces fragmented tech stacks.

The Problem

Restaurant operators juggle disconnected systems—POS, inventory, labor, reporting—that don't integrate. This creates messy data, manual reconciliation, and operational friction.

The Solution

One coherent platform that consolidates workflows and unifies operational data. Fewer systems. Smarter operations. Designed to work even when integrations fail.

Core Promise

ChiveIQ helps restaurant operators eliminate system sprawl, streamline workflows, and run their business with clarity and confidence.
