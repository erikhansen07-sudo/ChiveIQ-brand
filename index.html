Consolidate your fragmented tech stack. One platform for inventory, POS, recipes, and live P&L. Run your restaurant with clarity.

Brand Position

The Problem

Restaurant operators juggle disconnected systems—POS, inventory, labor, reporting—that don't integrate. This creates messy data, manual reconciliation, and operational friction.

The Solution

ChiveIQ consolidates workflows and unifies operational data into one intelligent platform. Fewer systems. Smarter operations. Designed to work even when integrations fail.

Core Promise

Help restaurant operators eliminate system sprawl, streamline workflows, and run their business with clarity and confidence.
