Logo Usage
On Paper — Primary
On Black — Acceptable
On Forest — Acceptable
Do
  • Use the full wordmark on all brand-facing applications
  • Maintain minimum clear space equal to cap-height on all sides
  • Present on paper, black, or dark green surfaces only
  • Always use source files — never recreate the wordmark
Don't
  • Alter letterform proportions or apply shadows or gradients
  • Use colors outside the approved brand palette
  • Place on busy photographic backgrounds
  • Stretch, skew, or recolor individual letterforms
08
Platform Modules

Six modules.
One system.

Each module is an integrated layer of the same operational OS — not a point solution bolted onto others.

Module 01
POS

Fast order entry, menu management, real-time cart, and checkout. Built for high-turnover environments with minimal training overhead.

Module 02
Inventory

Stock tracking, recipe costing, waste management, and reorder alerts. Works with messy, delayed data — because real inventory always is.

Module 03
Labor

Shift scheduling, labor cost tracking, forecasting, and department assignment. Enough lead time to act on numbers, not just observe them.

Module 04
Live P&L

Real-time revenue, COGS, labor cost, profit margin, and variance analysis. Not a retrospective — a live operating instrument.

Module 05
Operations Hub

Central KPI dashboard, alerts, recipe publishing, and historical tracking. The one screen that tells you where to focus across all locations.

Module 06
Analytics

Insights into sales, costs, trends, waste, and efficiency. Every data point should drive a decision — if it doesn't, it shouldn't exist.
