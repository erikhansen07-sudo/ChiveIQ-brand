05 / 11
Product
Seven domains. One platform.
Labor
Real-time labor cost as a % of sales — by shift, by location, by day. Overtime alerts fire before the violation, not after the payroll run.
Inventory
Variance tracking from order to shelf to waste log. Automated reorder triggers based on par levels and forecasted demand — no more clipboard guesswork.
P&L
Live profit & loss per location, updated intraday. Operators see margin impact of every decision in real time — not at month-end close.
Scheduling
AI-built schedules aligned to forecasted traffic, labor budgets, and compliance rules. Publish in minutes. Adjust with a tap when volume shifts.
Vendors
Centralized vendor scorecards, price-change alerts, and contract visibility. Compare costs across suppliers without leaving the platform.
Forecasting
Demand curves built from historical sales, weather, local events, and day-part patterns — giving operators a reliable forward view for staffing and ordering.
Menu
Item-level profitability, mix analysis, and pricing recommendations. Know which items are eroding margin and which to promote — continuously.
ChiveIQ Agent
The intelligence layer across all seven domains.
The Agent doesn't just surface data — it acts on it. It monitors every domain in real time, identifies deviations from target, and delivers plain-language interventions directly to the operator: "Your Tuesday lunch labor is running 4% over — here's an adjusted schedule." No dashboards required. No digging. The platform thinks with you.
